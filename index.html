Privacy Policy

Your safety and privacy are our foundation.

Your privacy is our priority

Still Space does not collect or share your personal information with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing partners. The app does not show ads and does not track you across other apps or websites.

Where your data lives

All your entries — mood journal, completed actions, chat history, settings — are stored on your iPhone or iPad and encrypted using AES-256-GCM (Apple CryptoKit). Encryption keys are stored in your iCloud Keychain and never leave Apple’s infrastructure.

If you are signed in to iCloud, your data may sync between your own devices through Apple’s CloudKit service. This sync happens entirely through Apple’s infrastructure — we have no access to your synced data and cannot read, share, or use it.

AI chat

Still Space includes an AI chat feature. When you send a message:

  1. Your message is sent through our service (hosted on Cloudflare) to Anthropic’s Claude API for processing.
  2. To help the AI remember context from past conversations, we may also send recent chat summaries and mood entries from your device.
  3. The AI’s response is sent back to you.

We do not permanently store your chat conversations on our servers. Cloudflare and Anthropic may temporarily process and log request metadata as part of standard service operation. Anthropic’s data practices are governed by their Privacy Policy.

Your full chat history is stored only on your device, encrypted. You can delete all chat data at any time in Settings → Delete all data.

Voice input

Still Space supports voice input for journaling and chat. Speech recognition is performed entirely on your device using Apple’s built-in Speech framework. No audio data is sent to our servers or any third party.

Apple Health

With your explicit permission, Still Space can read data from Apple Health (sleep, activity, menstrual cycle). This data is used only to personalize your recommendations within the app. Health data is never sent to any server, never shared with third parties, and never used for analytics. You can revoke access at any time in your device Settings.

Anonymous analytics (with your consent)

Still Space uses Amplitude to collect anonymous usage statistics — only with your explicit consent. We see which screens are opened, which actions are popular, and the general reasons you chose the app (e.g. stress, anxiety, sleep). This helps us improve the experience. No personal data or journal text is shared. You can disable analytics at any time in Settings.

Push notifications

Still Space may send local reminders to help you maintain your self-care routine. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to our servers. You can manage notification settings within the app or in your device Settings.

Subscriptions & Payments

Still Space offers an optional auto-renewable subscription managed entirely through Apple’s App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. All transactions are processed by Apple. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your device Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. For full terms, see Apple’s Standard EULA.

No registration or accounts

Still Space does not require creating an account, logging in via email or social networks. You just open the app and use it. No passwords, no profiles, no risk of data breaches.

Data deletion

Your data is stored on your device and may be synced via iCloud between your own devices. To permanently delete all data — including iCloud copies — use the “Delete all data” button in Settings before removing the app. If you simply delete the app, data stored in your iCloud account may remain. No data is ever stored on our servers.

Policy changes

If we make changes to this privacy policy, you will see the updated version in the app. We will never start collecting new types of data without your explicit consent.
