Privacy Policy

Your safety and privacy are our foundation.

Your privacy is our priority

Still Space does not collect or share your personal information with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing partners. The app does not show ads and does not track you across other apps or websites.

Where your data lives

All your entries — mood journal, completed actions, chat history, settings — are stored on your iPhone or iPad and encrypted using AES-256-GCM (Apple CryptoKit). Encryption keys are stored in your iCloud Keychain and never leave Apple’s infrastructure.

If you are signed in to iCloud, certain data may sync between your own devices through Apple’s iCloud Key-Value Storage. This sync happens entirely through Apple’s infrastructure — we have no access to your synced data and cannot read, share, or use it.

AI chat

Still Space includes an AI chat feature. When you send a message:

  1. Your message is sent through our service (hosted on Cloudflare) to Anthropic’s Claude API for processing.
  2. To help the AI remember context from past conversations, we may also send recent chat summaries and mood entries from your device.
  3. With each request, we send anonymous metadata: a randomly generated device identifier (used to enforce daily message limits), your app language, and your country (used to comply with regional requirements). This identifier is not linked to your name, email, or any personal account.
  4. The AI’s response is sent back to you.

We do not permanently store your chat conversations on our servers. Cloudflare and Anthropic may temporarily process and log request metadata as part of standard service operation. Anthropic’s data practices are governed by their Privacy Policy.

Your full chat history is stored only on your device, encrypted. You can delete all chat data at any time in Settings → Delete all data.

Voice input

Still Space supports voice input for journaling and chat. Speech recognition uses Apple’s built-in Speech framework.

  • For English: Speech recognition is performed entirely on your device.
  • For Russian and Ukrainian: Speech recognition is processed by Apple’s servers, as on-device recognition is not yet available for these languages on iOS. Apple’s data practices for Speech Recognition are described in Apple’s Privacy Policy.

We do not have access to your voice recordings or transcriptions. No audio data is sent to our servers.

Apple Health

With your explicit permission, Still Space can read data from Apple Health (sleep, activity, menstrual cycle). This data is used only to personalize your recommendations within the app. Health data is never sent to any server, never shared with third parties, and never used for analytics. You can revoke access at any time in your device Settings.

Anonymous analytics (with your consent)

Still Space uses Amplitude to collect anonymous usage statistics — only with your explicit consent given during onboarding.

We see:

  • Which screens are opened
  • Which features are used
  • The general reasons you indicated for choosing the app (e.g. stress, anxiety, sleep) — used in aggregate to improve features

We do NOT share:

  • Journal entries
  • Chat messages
  • Mood data
  • Any text you write

You can disable analytics at any time in Settings.

Push notifications

Still Space may send reminders to help you maintain your self-care routine. Most notifications are scheduled locally on your device. The app uses Apple Push Notification service (APNs) to support delivery of notifications even when the app is not running. We do not send notification content through APNs — only delivery triggers. No personal data is included in push notifications. You can manage notification settings within the app or in your device Settings.

Subscriptions & Payments

Still Space offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions managed entirely through Apple’s App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. All transactions are processed by Apple. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your device Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. For full terms, see Apple’s Standard EULA.

No registration required

Still Space does not require creating an account, logging in via email or social networks. You just open the app and use it. No passwords, no profiles.

Data deletion

Your data is stored on your device and may be synced via iCloud between your own devices. To permanently delete all data — including iCloud copies — use the “Delete all data” button in Settings before removing the app. If you simply delete the app, data synced to your iCloud account may remain. No data is ever stored on our servers.

Children

Still Space is intended for users 18 years or older (or the age of legal majority in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Policy changes

If we make changes to this privacy policy, you will see the updated version in the app. We will never start collecting new types of data without your explicit consent.
