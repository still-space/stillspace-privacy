Privacy Policy

Your safety and privacy are our foundation.

We don't collect your data

Still Space does not collect, store on its own servers, or share any personal information with third parties. The app does not use trackers, analytics systems, or advertising networks. Your privacy is our priority.

All data is stored on your device

All your entries — mood journal, completed actions, support notes, settings — are stored on your iPhone or iPad. If you are signed in to iCloud, your data may sync between your own devices via Apple’s iCloud Key-Value Storage. This sync happens entirely through Apple’s infrastructure — we have no access to this data and cannot see or use it.

No analytics or ads

Still Space does not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other analytics services. The app does not show ads and does not track your behavior.

No registration or accounts

Still Space does not require creating an account, logging in via email or social networks. You just open the app and use it. No passwords, no profiles, no risk of data breaches.

Data deletion

Since all data is stored on your device (and optionally synced via your personal iCloud account), you can completely delete it at any time by simply deleting the app. No traces will remain on our side.

Policy changes

If we make changes to this privacy policy, you will see the updated version in the app. We will never start collecting data without your explicit consent.
