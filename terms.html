What Still Space is

Still Space is a self-care companion app. It helps you check in with how you feel, suggests small actions, and gives you space to breathe, write, and reflect. It is not a medical device, not a substitute for therapy, and does not provide clinical advice.

Subscriptions

Still Space offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions (monthly and yearly) that unlock additional features. Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. Your subscription automatically renews unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period at the same price. You can manage and cancel your subscriptions in your device Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
