Simple and honest — like the app itself.

What Still Space is

Still Space is a self-care companion app. It helps you check in with how you feel, offers short practices when you need them, notices patterns over time, and gives you space to breathe, write, and reflect. It includes an AI chat where you can share what’s on your mind and put your thoughts into words.

Still Space is not a medical device, not a substitute for therapy or psychological care, and does not provide medical, clinical, or psychological advice. If you’re in crisis or need professional support, please reach out to a qualified provider or local crisis line — you can find resources in the app’s Support tab.

Subscriptions

Still Space offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions (monthly and yearly) that unlock additional features. Pricing is shown at the point of purchase. Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. Your subscription automatically renews unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period at the same price. You can manage and cancel your subscriptions in your device Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Free trial

Still Space may offer a free trial period. If you do not cancel before the trial ends, your subscription will begin and you will be charged. Any unused portion of a free trial is forfeited when you purchase a subscription.

Your content

Everything you create in Still Space — journal entries, mood data, chat messages — belongs to you. Your content is stored on your device, encrypted using AES-256-GCM, and optionally synced through your own iCloud account using Apple’s CloudKit. We do not have access to your encrypted content.

AI chat

Still Space includes an AI chat feature. Messages are sent through our service to Anthropic’s Claude API, which generates responses. We do not store your chat conversations on our servers.

The AI chat is a tool for personal reflection and conversation. It is not a therapist, counselor, or mental health professional. It does not provide diagnoses, treatment, advice, or assessments of any kind. Responses are generated by language models and may be inaccurate, incomplete, or unsuitable for your situation. Do not rely on the chat for crisis support — if you are in danger, contact emergency services immediately.

Not medical advice

Still Space is a wellness tool, not a healthcare provider. The app does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any medical or psychological condition. If you are in crisis, please contact emergency services or a helpline — support contacts are available in the app.
