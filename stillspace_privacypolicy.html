Privacy Policy

Your safety and privacy are our foundation.

We don't collect your data

StillSpace does not collect, store on servers, or share any personal information with third parties. The app does not use trackers, analytics systems, or advertising networks. Your privacy is our priority.

All data is stored locally

All your entries ‚Äî mood journal, completed actions, support notes, settings ‚Äî are stored exclusively on your iPhone or iPad. We have no access to this data and will never be able to see or use it.

No analytics or ads

StillSpace does not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other analytics services. The app does not show ads and does not track your behavior.

No registration or accounts

StillSpace does not require creating an account, logging in via email or social networks. You just open the app and use it. No passwords, no profiles, no risk of data breaches.

Data deletion

Since all data is stored only on your device, you can completely delete it at any time by simply deleting the app. No traces will remain.

Policy changes

If we make changes to this privacy policy, you will see the updated version in the app. We will never start collecting data without your explicit consent.
